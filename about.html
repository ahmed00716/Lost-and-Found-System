Our Mission

The Lost & Found Platform for University Students & Staff was created to solve the critical problem of inefficient property recovery within the university community.

The Challenge & The Solution

Problem Statement: Traditional methods for recovering lost items (social media, physical offices) rely on inefficient means, leading to difficulties in retrieving property and a low chance of finding it.

Our Solution: We provide a centralized, unified, and reliable web platform that allows users to securely report and browse lost or found properties, fulfilling the need to facilitate and accelerate the return process.

Key Features

  • Centralized Reporting for Lost and Found Items.
  • Secure Communication between the finder and owner (via email display after login).
  • Smart Matching (AI) based on item description and category.
  • Admin Oversight for managing users and ensuring platform integrity.
